Borderlands Hex Multitool

How to use:
1) Use the "Browse..."-button and select your games executable
2) Select your desired options down below
3) Click the "Save"-button and "download" your modified exe-file and replace it with your own
4) Profit

* Note: Example locations for your exe:
C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Borderlands 2\Binaries\Win32\Borderlands 2.exe
C:\Program Files (x86)\Epic Games\Borderlands2\Binaries\Win32\Borderlands 2.exe

**Note: This does NOT edit your WillowInput.ini-file to set a ConsoleKey!
***Note: Currently, only Windows executables are supported!

All of this is still in beta! It is not guaranteed to work for everyone. If you experience any issues, try in a different browser or refresh with CTRL + F5
